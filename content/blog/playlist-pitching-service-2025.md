## What is Playlist Pitching?

Playlist pitching is the process of submitting your music to playlist curators for consideration. If they like your track, they may add it to their playlist.

## Why It Works

- Reach new listeners in your genre
- Build streaming momentum
- Trigger algorithmic recommendations
- Get real feedback on your music
