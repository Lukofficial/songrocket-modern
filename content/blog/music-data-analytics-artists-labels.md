Data analytics is crucial for making smart decisions about your music career. Here's how to use it effectively.

## Key Metrics to Track

- Monthly listeners and trends
- Stream sources (playlists, albums, search)
- Listener demographics and geography
- Save rates and skip rates
- Playlist additions and removals

## Tools to Use

- **Spotify for Artists:** Essential for Spotify data
- **Chartmetric:** Cross-platform analytics
- **Soundcharts:** Industry-level insights
- **SongRocket:** Campaign performance tracking
