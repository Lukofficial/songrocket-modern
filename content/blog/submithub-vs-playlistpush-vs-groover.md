Detailed comparison of the top playlist promotion platforms.

## SubmitHub

- Credit-based system
- Blogs, playlists, and influencers
- Guaranteed response times

## PlaylistPush

- Spotify-focused
- Higher minimum budgets
- Curator feedback included

## Groover

- European-focused
- 7-day response guarantee
- Industry contacts included

## SongRocket

- Lowest entry point ($3)
- Manual curator vetting
- Campaign extensions
- Real-time dashboard
