Essential marketing strategies every artist needs for 2025.

## 1. Short-Form Video

TikTok and Instagram Reels are non-negotiable. Create 15-60 second clips.

## 2. Playlist Pitching

Still one of the most effective discovery channels.

## 3. Collaboration

Cross-promote with artists in your lane.

## 4. Email Marketing

Build direct relationships outside social media algorithms.

## 5. Live Streaming

Real-time engagement builds superfans.

## 6. Community Building

Discord servers and fan clubs for your most dedicated listeners.
