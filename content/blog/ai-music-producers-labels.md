AI is transforming the music industry. Here's what producers and labels need to know.

## AI in Production

- AI-assisted mixing and mastering
- Sample generation and sound design
- Vocal processing and tuning
- Stem separation tools

## AI for Labels

- A&R discovery tools
- Trend prediction and analytics
- Playlist optimization
- Marketing automation

## The Human Element

AI is a tool, not a replacement. The best results come from combining AI efficiency with human creativity and judgment.
