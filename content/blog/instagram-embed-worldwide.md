Instagram's embed feature is now available worldwide, opening new opportunities for music promotion.

## How It Works

You can now embed Instagram posts and Reels directly on websites, blogs, and other platforms.

## For Artists

- Embed music Reels on your website
- Share posts on fan sites and blogs
- Cross-promote with partner sites
