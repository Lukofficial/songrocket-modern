Choosing the right distributor is crucial. Here's the 2025 comparison.

## Top Options

- **DistroKid:** $22.99/year, unlimited releases, fast
- **TuneCore:** Per-release pricing, established
- **CD Baby:** One-time fee, good for physical
- **Amuse:** Free tier available
- **United Masters:** Artist-focused features

## What to Consider

- Pricing model (subscription vs per-release)
- Revenue split (keep 100% of royalties)
- Platform reach
- Additional features
