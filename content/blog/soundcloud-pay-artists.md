SoundCloud has its own monetization program for artists. Here's what you need to know about earnings.

## SoundCloud Partner Program

SoundCloud pays artists through its Partner Program, which includes:

- Fan-powered royalties (earnings based on your specific listeners)
- SoundCloud Go+ subscription revenue share
- Advertising revenue from free tier

## How Much Can You Earn?

Earnings vary significantly based on your audience's location and listening behavior. Estimates range from $0.0025 to $0.004 per stream on average.

## Eligibility Requirements

- SoundCloud Pro or Pro Unlimited subscription
- Original content only
- Meet minimum monetization thresholds
