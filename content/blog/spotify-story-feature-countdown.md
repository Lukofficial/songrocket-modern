Spotify's story feature and countdown tools help artists promote upcoming releases. Here's how to use them.

## Countdown Feature

Available through Spotify for Artists, the countdown feature lets you build anticipation before a release:

- Add to any scheduled release
- Fans can save the release date
- Appears on your artist profile

## Best Practices

- Promote the countdown on social media
- Use Spotify Canvas for visual engagement
- Submit to Spotify Editorial early
