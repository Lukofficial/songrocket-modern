YouTube remains a powerful platform for music discovery. Here's how to optimize your presence.

## Setting Up Official Artist Channel

Claim your Official Artist Channel through your distributor to access special features:

- Unified channel across multiple uploads
- Artist profile and bio
- Music shelf with all your releases

## Content Strategy

- Music videos
- Lyric videos
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Live performances
- Shorts for discovery
