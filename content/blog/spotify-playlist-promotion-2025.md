The complete guide to promoting your music through Spotify playlists in 2025.

## Types of Playlists

- **Editorial:** Curated by Spotify's team
- **Algorithmic:** Personalized like Discover Weekly
- **User/Third-party:** Created by independent curators

## Strategy

1. Submit to Spotify Editorial 4 weeks early
2. Use SongRocket for third-party playlists
3. Create and promote your own playlists
4. Engage with curators on social media
