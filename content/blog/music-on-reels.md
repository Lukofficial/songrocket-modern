Instagram Reels is a powerful tool for music promotion. Here's how to use it effectively.

## Content Ideas

- Song snippets with lyrics
- Behind-the-scenes studio footage
- Performance clips
- Artist day-in-the-life
- Fan reactions and user-generated content

## Best Practices

- Post consistently (daily or every other day)
- Use trending audio when relevant
- Hook viewers in the first second
- Include calls-to-action

## Driving to Spotify

Use your bio link to direct Reels viewers to your latest release on Spotify.
